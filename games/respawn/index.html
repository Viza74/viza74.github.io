Kill all monsters on the screen!
Unfortunately you don't have any weapons... But you have the gift of explosive rebirth.
So just click to die, and when in your angel form, click again to respawn - and explode!
Any monster in the explosion radius will explode too, creating a chain reaction if you are lucky.
A couple of more rules though:
You have limited number of respawns, use them wisely!
When you die, the ground gets scarred - you can't respawn on scarred ground.
The radius of your respawns' explosion is determined by how many monster are around you when you die.
So the strategy is more-or-less: Let the monster close in on you, and in the last possible second, click to die.
