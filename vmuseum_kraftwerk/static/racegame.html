Solar Energy

Energy directly from the sun

Solar power is pollution free and causes no greenhouse gases to be emitted after installation. It has reduced dependence on oil and other fossil fuels. It is a renewable and clean power that is available every day of the year and in abundance in Saudi Arabia.

Solar panels are installed in millions of home and they can be used from running a single street lamp to an entire home. Solar energy is used for water heating, air conditioners, lights and the list goes on. Photovoltaic cells are no more as expensive as it used to be and maintenance is easy and most importantly, we become ambassadors of a green tomorrow.
