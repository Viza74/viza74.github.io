Power Efficiency

The same result - with less power consumption

Bulbs have been around for centuries and we have only been improvising them. Today they vary with size, colour, brightness and power required. Do we really know the implications of power requirement or the variances of LED bulbs?

Little fact:
If you pedal for an hour a day, 30 days a month, that's (30 x 100=) 3000 watt-hours, or 3 kWh and that's less than 1% of what a typical family uses in a month (920 kWH). You generated 0.3% of your energy, and continue to get 99.7% from the grid.
